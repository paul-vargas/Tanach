Maurice T. Boisvert, Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu, Quebec, Canada, suggested links to other sites and motivated this option.

The internet abounds with biblical texts and information of varying quality and provenance. The viewer is his own best judge of such internet materials. This page describes how view the site's built-in external links.

There's no endorsement of any of these links.

Links are shown in a pulldown list on the bottom, left corner of the book pages. Each site name appears in a pull down list. With a link chosen from the pulldown, press the adjacent "Go" button to view the external link in a separate window.

The transcriber welcomes suggestions for additional external links.
